Glossary

noun: A list of often difficult or specialized words with their definitions, often placed at the back of a book.

  • Data Breach: The unauthorized access or disclosure of confidential information, often leading to financial and reputational damage.
  • Secret Management: The practice of securely storing, accessing, and sharing sensitive information such as passwords, API keys, and certificates.
  • Vault: A system or software designed for securely managing secrets and access controls for applications.
  • MORE.Tech Hackathon: An innovation event where teams develop prototypes and solutions, in this case, hosted by VTB.
  • HashiCorp Vault: A popular commercial tool for secure secret management, providing encryption and access control features.
  • Encryption: The process of converting information into a coded format to prevent unauthorized access.
  • RESTful API: An application programming interface (API) that follows REST principles, allowing applications to communicate seamlessly over the web.
  • Latency: The delay or time taken for a system to process a request and respond.
  • Seamless Integration: The ability to incorporate a tool or system into existing applications without significant effort or disruption.
